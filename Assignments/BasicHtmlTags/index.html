Gaming Pc

A look at high end gaming pcs

There are multiple factors that go in to building a high end pc suitable for gaming. There are many components needed to get started Lets look at the major components and see what whats needed to get started

Theres a long list of parts needed when constructing a pc build but there are major compnents that really need to addressed first and where most of your money should go. The main parts are
  • Motherboard
  • gpu
  • Memory
  • cpu
  • memory
  • hard drive
  • power supply
  • case
  • monitor
  • Mouse and keyboard

There are many more pieces you can get but theres just some you cant live without. You can go from the low end and spend around 1500 dollars or if your budgets high enough you could easily sppend upwards of 5000 dollars. Tech Buyers Guru is a good site to get started if you arent familiar with components. Building a gaming pc all depends on your dreams but mostly depends on your budget. Good luck and happy building!
